We are

Delivering the right career to the right candidate at the right time

We are CareerCentric. CareerCentric drives qualified candidates to employment opportunities and helps employers fulfill strategic hiring objectives. Through the use of behavioral data and matching technology, CareerCentric delivers relevant opportunities to job seekers through email and mobile engagements on our network of job search destinations.

CareerCentric engages job seekers across the full lifecycle of their careers. Whether a candidate is researching their next big career move; passively exploring new opportunities; or seeking a part time job, CareerCentric provides candidates with access to the most relevant opportunities.

Results

CareerCentric partners with employers to drive qualified candidates that meet their target performance metrics. Our data driven platform blends content relevancy algorithms with a pay for performance model to maximize the value of each job seeker interaction and ensure that candidates are qualified when they apply. These results are possible thanks to our comprehensive understanding of job seeker lifecycles and search behaviors derived from the CareerCentric ecosystem.

Our Audience

CareerCentric serves 18,000,000 job seeker visits each month, covering a large percentage of both active and passive US job seekers. Leveraging over 100,000,000 job seeker profiles, we intelligently engage with passive job seekers, introducing relevant content to the most qualified candidates who are not actively reviewing job opportunities. CareerCentric adaptively segments audience members to ensure that job seekers receive the right career message at each step of their search.

Our Brands

Our brands are the consumer touchpoints of CareerCentric and provide the channels to engage job seekers with targeted career content. This branded approach allows a high level of customization for specific audience segments that would not be possible with a one size fits all methodology. With these brands we continue to build rich user experiences that not only improve the job search process for our users but also enable us to deliver content that empowers people to change their lives.

The CareerCentric Network was developed by Restoration Media, an Inc. 5000 company.
